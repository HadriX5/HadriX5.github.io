My Journey

I've always believed that the best software isn't just about solving a logical puzzle—it's about building something that lasts. While I have a strong grasp of algorithmic foundations, I find my true rhythm when I'm designing robust architectures and crafting well-structured databases. To me, there's a unique satisfaction in choosing the right design pattern or optimizing a schema to ensure a system remains clean and scalable.

As a Computer Engineering student at the University of Barcelona, my focus has naturally gravitated towards the intersection of Software Engineering and Data Science. I'm the kind of developer who prioritizes the long-term maintainability of a 4-layer mobile ecosystem over competing in abstract algorithmic contests. I thrive when I can transform complex data into smooth, efficient, and intuitive user experiences.

Beyond the code, I value grit, transparency, and the continuous process of learning. I am currently seeking my first professional opportunity where I can contribute to a team that values solid engineering principles, learn from experienced mentors, and build tools that have a tangible impact on the real world.

I value the rigor of mathematical theory, but my goal is always its tangible application. From optimizing database schemas to refining ML models, I strive for the precision that results in efficient, real-world software solutions.
